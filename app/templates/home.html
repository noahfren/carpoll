What is carpoll?

carpoll helps everybody get involved in government. Our goal is to eliminate the excuses for not voting and make democracy accesible to all, regarldess of access to transportation.

How does it work?

It's simple! Just signup to get a ride, enter your name, email address, zipcode and choose a time you would like to vote. We'll put you in contact you with one of our volunteer drivers and you can arrange a pickup location. After you vote, you can arrange to have the driver take you back to the pickup location.

Cool! How can I help?

Glad you asked! carpoll relies on volunteer drivers who sign up to give someone a ride to and from a polling place. Simply signup to drive at the top of the page and well put you in contact with someone who needs a ride. We'll make sure they are at least within your zipcode's distance. You will be expected to drive your passanger back to the pickup location after you vote.
